Ping Pong Game











Developed By: Sohan Thakur



Instructions

  1. First Keep Your Laptop Screen Straight.
  2. Move Yourself Approximately 3-4 Feet Away From Screen.
  3. Move Your Right Wrist In An Ascending and Descending Manner.
  4. Press The Play Button
  5. The Red Paddle Is Your Paddle, That Will Move As Per Your Right Wrist Movement's.
